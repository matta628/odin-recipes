Recipe to Get Whacked (by Tony)

Description

Feeling depressed after an unfortunate incident involving your son and a bow and arrow? Sick of taking the capo's shit all the time? Has all the coke you've done addled your brain? Try getting whacked by Tony Soprano! Just follow our steps

Ingredients

  • An animal, preferably a horse
  • An insurance plan for said animal
  • A smart mouth
  • The audacity

Steps

  1. First make Tony get emotionally attached to an animal he ends up taking off your hands
  2. After you find yourself in a bind for some cash, choose a nice insurance plan for your animal
  3. Be unreachable when a fire unfortunate burns down the stables where your horse lives
  4. Seem happy to receive the insurance money when Tony lets you know the sad news
  5. Dodge his questions and pose your own: What are you a horse investigator now?
  6. Say the horse (or other animal) was just meat
  7. Call Tony fat
  8. Enjoy the afterlife!
