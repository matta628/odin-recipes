Recipe for Success!

Description

Sick and tired of despair? Have you had enough of being hopeless? Want to change your circumstances? Well then we've got a delicious recipe for you :)

Ingredients

  • Acceptance
  • Discipline
  • Long term mindset
  • Patience

Steps

  1. Forgive yourself for your past actions and realize you did the best you could
  2. Realize you're not the only one who's struggling to find a job right now
  3. Pat yourself on the back for having at least tried and gotten close with multiple companies
  4. Everytime you fail or don't meet your goals, don't berate yourself, just self correct without judgement
  5. Think positively and attract goodness
